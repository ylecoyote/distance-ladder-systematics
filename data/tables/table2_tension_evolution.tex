Stage 2: SH0ES total uncertainty ($\sigma_{\rm sys} = 1.04$ km~s$^{-1}$~Mpc$^{-1}$, uncorrelated). Stage 3: Parallax zero point (Scenario A baseline: adopt SH0ES internally-fitted ZP with no additional bias; H$_0$ remains 73.04 km~s$^{-1}$~Mpc$^{-1}$ from \citealt{Riess2022}).
